Crowd Analyzer

The idea of the project originated from a real life problem. Usually it is difficult to predict when the rush is more in the badminton court or at the gym. This results in employees going to gym or badminton court at wrong times when there is more rush and end up waiting for their turn. Crowd Analyser addresses this problem by tracking people entering into and exiting from an arena and continuously publishing results here. As it provides (near) realtime results, it helps employees plan their activities in a better way.
